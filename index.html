Humans live on Mars
is it possible?

Living on Mars presents numerous challenges, including its thin, unbreathable atmosphere, high radiation levels, extreme cold, and scarcity of liquid water. However, advances in technology could potentially overcome these hurdles through the development of life support systems, radiation-shielded habitats, and robotic assistance for infrastructure.
